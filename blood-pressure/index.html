Privacy Policy for Blood Pressure App

Last updated: 2026-05-08

Blood Pressure Note App respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

This app does not collect or transmit any personal data.

All data you enter, such as blood pressure readings and notes, is stored locally on your device only.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely within the app to help you record and track your blood pressure.

We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

4. Permissions

This app does not require access to sensitive device features such as contacts, location, camera, or microphone.

5. Advertising

This app may display advertisements provided by third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect limited, non-personal information (such as device data) to provide and improve ads.

6. Your Consent

By using this app, you agree to this privacy policy.
