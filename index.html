Privacy Policy for Noise Detector

Last updated: 2026-05-08

Noise Detector respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle audio data collected by the app.

1. Information We Collect

The app may access your device’s microphone to record audio when you use features that require it. We do not collect or store audio recordings unless explicitly used for app functionality.

2. How We Use Your Data

Audio data is used solely to provide the features you request within the app. We do not share, sell, or distribute your audio data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Any audio data temporarily used by the app is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to external servers.

4. Your Consent

By using the app, you consent to the access and temporary use of your microphone for app functionality.
